The First Fleet and the

Colonization of Australia

Having lost the American colonies, the British arrival in Botany Bay in western Australia in 1788 was the next step in the development of the British Empire. The search for replacement colonies and the quest for new resources and territory led the British and other Europeans into competition and conflict with the natives of these newly discovered lands. The effects of the colonization of Australia are still felt to this day.
